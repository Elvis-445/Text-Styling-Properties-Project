Facts About Lamborghini

Lamborghini was founded by Ferruccio Lamborghini, who initially made tractors before venturing into luxury sports cars.

The company’s logo features a charging bull, reflecting Ferruccio’s zodiac sign, Taurus, and his passion for bullfighting.

The company is known for its innovative designs and powerful engines, including the V12 and V10 engines.
